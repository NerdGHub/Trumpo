1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Trumpo Limited ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

By continuing to use our site, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy, unless you choose to disable them in your browser settings.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites recognise your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

We use both:

  • Session cookies – temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies – cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to support:

  • Essential functionality – to ensure our website works properly (e.g., login and form submissions).
  • Performance & analytics – to understand how visitors use our website and improve performance.
  • Personalisation – to remember your preferences and settings.
  • Advertising & marketing – to deliver relevant ads and track campaign effectiveness.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Examples
Strictly Necessary Required for website functionality. Cannot be switched off. Session management, security cookies.
Performance & Analytics Help us measure website traffic and usage. Google Analytics, site performance tools.
Functionality Remember your choices (e.g., language, region). Preference settings.
Targeting & Advertising Track browsing habits to deliver relevant ads. Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, affiliate tracking cookies.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use, such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising networks (e.g., Google Ads, social media platforms).
  • Referral management tools used to connect you with financial service providers.

We do not control third-party cookies, and we recommend reviewing their own privacy and cookie policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Instructions are available on the support pages for major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Apple Safari

Please note, disabling cookies may affect the functionality and performance of our website.

7. Consent to Cookies

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner. By clicking "Accept" or continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies in line with this policy. You can change your preferences at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices, technology, or legal obligations. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
