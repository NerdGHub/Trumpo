Overview

Trumpo.com operates a transparent financial model with key on-chain and off-chain reporting. Our ecosystem includes direct exposure to Bitcoin through a long-term treasury strategy, income from P2P lending, and a rewards-based utility token system.

Bitcoin Treasury Strategy:

Trumpo.com follows a Michael Saylor-inspired Bitcoin-backed treasury model. We use low cost debt financing (3% perpetual loans) to acquire and hold BTC for long-term value preservation and growth.

This strategy supports:

  • Inflation protection
  • Yield via performance-linked structures
  • BTC dividends or potential BIPS airdrops

Perpetual 3% Loan Model:

We offer ISA and pension investors access to a 3% per annum perpetual P2P loan product via JustUs.

Structured performance-based tiers can increase or decrease yield based on:
  • 25%, 50%, and 75% uplift/downlift based on market metrics
  • BTC price triggers and lending activity benchmarks
