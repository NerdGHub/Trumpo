The BiPS Exchange Token is a digital currency tacitly referenced to property and assets. It is an unregulated Exchange Token. The BiPS Exchange Token is used to securely remit, store or trade globally on blockchain Token exchanges. This may include Fiat (GBP, USD, Euro+++) or other digital currencies, using either BiPS or other Ethereum digital wallet.

You can buy any amount of BiPS and send them globally in under a second, with a transaction fee of just one penny.

95% of the value of BiPS is used by the BiPS Foundation to purchase real-world assets like property and commodities. These assets are held permanently to back the currency, giving BiPS intrinsic value and stability.
